LIL’ LEADER PROGRAM INFORMATION

Our academy offers several different age groups based on grade level as well as experience level allowing students to feel comfortable and more capable of achieving excellence in a fun and safe learning environment. The Lil’ Leader program is designed specifically for pre-school and kindergarten aged children. Class opportunities are offered for the Lil’ Leader program 4 times throughout the week to allow for a flexible training schedule; each class is 30 minutes in length and we recommend for students to attend 1-2 classes per week for ultimate results. Each class throughout the week has a specific theme for the students to benefit from through interactive discussions and drills; themes include respect, leadership, fitness, discipline, commitment and many more.

Participation in the Lil’ Leader program benefits the child’s elementary classroom experience by developing social skills, classroom manners and leadership skills. Children will be more prepared to participate in classroom discussions and activities with confidence they build at our academy.

MARTIAL ARTS TRAINING

Students begin by learning the basic stances, blocks, strikes, kick and escape skills. At each belt level, students are introduced to new material which builds off of previously learned techniques. Your child will have the opportunity to test for a new rank five times per year; this breaks down to five 10-12 week training cycles. Each week in the training cycle has a specific training theme such as memory, coordination, teamwork and many others. Students earn 5 curriculum stripes on each belt for learning specific techniques based on their age and experience level. The success of your child in our program is dependent on consistent training and focus on their goal to earn their TAEKWONDO BLACK BELT!

LEADERSHIP MAT CHATS

We use a few minutes in each class to do an interactive discussion based on a specific character development skill. Each week there is a different aspect of that skill for the instructors to discuss with the students. Students enjoy participating in these motivating conversations about:
  • Leadership - leading by example even when no one is watching
  • Strength - control over your actions as well as physical strength
  • Commitment - never give up even things get hard
  • Knowledge - learning is the key to success
  • Respect - treating others as you want to be treated

BIG TIME BENEFITS

Focus/Concentration - Children learn how to focus all of their attention to the task at hand while committing themselves to learning and growing.
Perseverance/Teamwork - Your child will learn how to face challenges presented to them, knowing that the challenges will be difficult to complete. They will learn how to face challenges alone and with peers.
Courage - Students learn how to overcome fear and nervousness through training that initially seems intimidating.
Discipline/Integrity - Lil’ Leaders perform their martial arts skills with strong effort and attitude.
Coordination/Speed - Your child will learn the components of speed and how to control their balance, body posture, and technique.
Fitness/Agility - Students learn the mechanics that govern movement and how to plays a role when they are jumping, dodging, and moving about quickly.
Control/Technique - Children learn the fundamental components of good technique, which will make them great athletes.
Flexibility - Your child will gain and maintain flexibility through fun and educational drills
